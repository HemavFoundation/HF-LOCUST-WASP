Locust Projet

The desert locust plagues are one of the most dangerous and destructives in the world, especially because of the damage caused to the food and agriculture resources.

This project is aimed to design, develop and implement drone and AI technology to strengthen the current Desert Locust early warning system and preventive control strategy with focusing firstly on the detection of risk areas and inspection of pre-swarm locust occurrences.
